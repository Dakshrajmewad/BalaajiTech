Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can browse our products, click on the "Buy Now" button, and you'll be redirected to WhatsApp to complete your order. Alternatively, you can call us directly or send us an email with your requirements.

What are your delivery timelines?

We typically deliver within 3-5 business days across major cities in India. Remote locations may take 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for products in their original condition and packaging. If you receive a defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order, and we'll arrange for a replacement or refund.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, most of our products come with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Specific warranty information is mentioned in the product details. Batteries and consumable components typically have a shorter warranty period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery (for orders under ₹2000). For WhatsApp orders, we'll provide payment instructions after confirming your order.
